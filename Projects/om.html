MEDITATION

Meditation, a practice that has spread worldwide and is now embraced by people from various cultures and traditions, has deep roots in the ancient spiritual and philosophical traditions of India, particularly the Sanatan Dharma, which encompasses Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism.

Yoga and Meditation Centers

The establishment of yoga and meditation centers, both in India and abroad, has played a significant role in spreading these practices offer classes, workshops, and retreats to individuals from diverse backgrounds.

Mindfulness Meditation

Mindfulness meditation, rooted in Buddhist Vipassana practices, has gained popularity globally for its focus on present-moment awareness and stress reduction. It is often used in clinical and therapeutic settings.

Yoga

Yogic meditation, as outlined in texts like the Yoga Sutras of Patanjali, is a central aspect of yoga and has been embraced worldwide as a means to achieve physical and mental well-being.
